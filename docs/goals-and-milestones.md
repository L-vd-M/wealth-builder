# Goals And Milestones

## Strategic Goals
1. Build an integrated market intelligence workspace.
2. Reduce analysis-to-trade decision time.
3. Support rapid quant strategy prototyping with AI.
4. Provide multi-asset visibility (crypto, forex, stocks).
